# RIPA: Act 3 Part 1
## Session 86 (2026-03-31)
### Campaign Day 37 / Act 3 Day 1, The Outlands, At Our Keep

We awaken in the keep, at slightly different times, in separate rooms.

* Urth wants to summon Flurth (and in due course we all agree, tho it'll take 12h)
* Penny goes to Francesc to chat / decompress
* Thorin decides to have breakfast!

Thorin goes down to the ground floor, looking for food. It's an opportunity to catch up with everyone *else* including Gundtach. Inda, the captain of the Lambent Zenith, is still here too. Gertrude is outside along with Redbud. Eventually we're all catching up - they have a lot of questions.

No Mayastan. Penny can't remember... "Does anyone have Sending?" - no; the people at the Keep always relied on Mayastan for that!

Inda's take; she wants to find out more! "I've been in similar circumstances before. There's always a resistance. There are always survivors who will be mobilising and we're not connected to that! "I feel disempowered". She wants to find enough people to basically raise or join a resistance army to take "the stronghold" - she views that shattered city as such. That spinning thing "moved in", they *saw* it happen.

She wants to just have permission to leave ("of course!") and get to a nearby city to start asking questions. She doesn't need any assistance. She says goodbye to Figaro, her First Mate who's still here; if we have need we can summon her; and in a rather abrupt way, in some respects, she's gone.

In ongoing conversations - who wants to leave or stay? - Penny INS 27, Redbud, the elven druid and the Blink Dog would like to stay - Mynos too, Gertrude, not-Alfred and so-on - but others are pledged somewhat similarly to Inda and will stay if we need them to stay, otherwise they'd like to pursue some form of justice. The planes call upon them! They can fight as part of what we're doing, or something else.

The four of us agree; the TL;DR is - go where your heart takes you and, if we can help, we'll help. But they'll hold onto a decision as they're not sure what *we* are going to do. Is the Keep a place for them to be helpful, or really just a place to stay?

If only we had Sending. We can't contact anyone externally! But Gundtach thinks we'd be able to from Trade Gate. Maybe we go there. Inda's going to a bar to try and overhear Resistance conversations! Well, that could be it.

But what of the bag? We never did check it. We do now!

It's full of flawless black opals. It takes a while to count... 750 of them. Thorin estimates each is worth 1000gp. So that's 750,000 gold equivalent.

"Well if we have a reason to own one, we *could* buy a ship, if there are any left" remarks Penny. Thorin thinks we should "gear up" - it might be a big battle we're getting into. But we certainly want Flurth in that case, so it means we'll stay at the keep today.

Gundtach wants to get moving and heads away too - but not Trade Gate; he wants to go to Limbo! And Gertrude wants to head away to find local giants, which is perhaps a more emotional departure, but we wish her well of course.

**Two fate die - a D8 for Urth, a D6 for Thorin.**

* Thorin: D6 -> 1
* Urth: D8 -> 2

Thorin starts asking about things in Trade Gate and recruiting help for people to look for things. He thinks there's something called a "Barrier Tattoo"? And the overall opinion is that Trade Gate has at least one such individual. A couple of people head out to verify and a few hours later they're back, complete with an address!

That afternoon:

* Thorin: D6 -> 5
* Urth: D8 -> 3

Francesc is in a garden outside the moved keep, Penny with her. Francesc PER 16, Penny ADV PER 18. Penny gets a strange feeling - hairs on the back of the neck - she draws the sword and lights it, which prompts Francesc to adopt a defensive pose but she doesn't know what it *actually* is and neither does Penny... "There's *something*" is all Penny can say. She tries using Divine Sense. Nothing. She starts to move away from the keep, directly away from the curved wall, to see if anything comes up. It feels as if clouds are gathering around her - oh, they actually are! - the weather around the keep is rapidly changing. Then over in a Western direction, a presence; strong, undead. She shouts a warning and turns to face where it *should* be. Initiative!

* Francesc 17
* Penny 16

Francesc gets a slight advantage by being within the aura range. She can't see much and asks Penny where it would be - "within 60ft!" - and elects to try casting Wind Wall, about 30ft away, as pretty much a defensive move. But she doesn't know much about what she's meant to be seeing.

Penny can see a strange red mist beyond the wind wall, and tries Sacred Flame on it; DEX 20 save; it fails, 4d8 -> 17 radiant. The mist kind of lights up. The mist comes up towards the wind wall then rises up and transforms into a humanoid. It seems to just sort of shrug aside the wind wall at that point and comes straight through! Penny PER 14... It's a vampire! *That's* why the weather was changed to overcast.

The scary vampire gives a menacing stare, direct at Francesc - but she's within range of Penny's aura so it does nothing. And at that point, Penny thinks - **KAS** - this is Kas! Back for the rod! She calls out as much.

Thorin PER 27. He's noticed it going dark and looks out of the window - and sees immediately that it's not natural; a thick bank of cloud surrounding just the tower. DEX save from Penny and Francesc; 17 and 25 respectively. Penny takes full damage and Francesc half, from a bolt of lightning that crashes down from the above. 30/15.

Thorin jumps out of the window - intending to transform as he goes, changing into a dragon - Shape Change, the spell - he's seen a Red Dragon before. But he clips the window sill on the way and the entire window is ripped out of the Keep.

Meanwhile, Francesc (DM fiat) first briefly uses Hunter's Mark to learn about Kas, then uses the rod - Reverse Gravity! The Wind Wall is gone. She tells Penny:

* Kas cannot be charmed, frightened, poisoned or paralysed and can't gain exhaustion.
* He is immune to necrotic, poison and has resistance to bludgeoning, piercing and slashing.

Thorin now sees the sight of lots of gravel and rocks and twigs starting to fly "upwards", with a strange character in the middle of it, Penny with sword drawn at high brightness, and to try and warn Urth - Thorin *roars*, very loudly. Urth is from Krynn! He knows *that* sound. "Dammit! I just needed a few more hours!" he curses and gives up on his Flurth spell.

He runs to a nearby window in the direction of the sound, in time to see a red dragon (why is that here?!) trying to take to the air, yet it seems somehow clumsy - and it's wearing large-size clothing! Thorin's belt, Thorin's black dragon-scale breastplate, but all at full size dragon scale. Urth ARC 22 - Ah! That *is* Thorin, using some new spell to shape-shift. Aaaaand - he wants to jump on its back!

ADV ATHL - 27. He opens the window, backs up a bit, then runs and LEAPS. He's dreamed of this moment, running off cliffs to jump onto a red dragon... And kill it, but this is good enough! Thorin feels the small character land on his back and makes a decent guess about who this is.

Initiative: Thorin 17, Urth 11

Kas, Legendary Action - he turns into a bat! He's trying to fly and not be thrown by the reverse-gravity. Penny tries Hold Monster - WIS 20 save - he succeeds with a 30. Rats. The sun sword is now at full brightness, emitting sunlight for a 30ft bright and 30ft dim range. The bat tries to fly in but seems to want to avoid that light, though it's not harming it apparently. But because it wasn't harmed before, it heals for 20hp.

Eventually the apparently-frustrated bat lands, turning back into Kas. He draws his sword, points it at Francesc and utters some kind of curse. A sickly black/green ray extends out and strikes her. Finger Of Death. CON save - lucky! 17+1+5 (aura) -> 23, matching DC, so she takes 61->30 necrotic damage. Kas turns his head and glares straight at the red dragon... Thorin, WIS save 15. Unfortunately, he becomes frightened! But Urth feels that and Reacts with Countercharm. Re-roll save 22! It succeeds.

Urth shoots an arrow; 27 to hit, hits; piercing, 8->4 damage; the as a second attack uses cantrip Firebolt via special ability, with Luck point, 17 to hit, which sadly does not hit. Then he sends to Bardic Inspiration to Jesse.

Francesc reapplies Hunter's Mark and fires at Kas; 19 would hit, but Kas Parries. Second attack - exactly the same again! She moves around behind Penny a little, using her as a shield (in a good way...) and avoiding casting a shadow from the sword's light with her own body in the direction of Kas. Kas meanwhile is doing something *weird*. From the ground, rises a spectre.

Thorin flies in to get close to Penny and be within the bright sunlight of the sword; the shadows he cast are cast upwards, since he's flying. He uses his fire breath to hit both Kas and Spectre. It'll ruin the garden, but he can regrow it! DEX 21 save. Failed by Kas and the Spectre, but Kas "chooses" to succeed. Potential 53 Spectre -> 26 Kas damage... And the spectre disappears.

"Betrayer!" shouts Jesse. "You'll not steal from us again! What did you do with Mayastan?!"

"Ha! That weak scholar? The naïve, ignorant, inexperienced scholar? The one who invites a vampire into its own home? You mean that one? I buried him in the back. And that's where I'll bury all of *you*. **Give me back my rod!**"

Penny knows she should protect Francesc but she's only good in melee; she runs up and swings; hits solidly. Undead. 6d8 + 10. Low roll, but 33, but radiant. Swings again, hits again; only 3d8 + 10 but this time a high roll, 29 damage. It's enough to break his concentration on the clouds. No more lightning!

Kas uses a Legendary Action yet again and summons another spectre; but he's in full bright sunlight at this point and, at the start of his turn (now) he takes 20 more damage and since he took radiant and is in sunlight, can't heal. He retreats! As he goes, Penny gets an attack of opportunity - 19 to hit; he parries, so no damage, but he's used his reaction. He holds the sword and Commands the red dragon to Yield ("Dominate Monster").

Thorin WIS save, 29! He resists easily.

Kas is no longer in sunlight and turns into mist; it looks like he's trying to escape. "Don't let him get away! -" shouts Penny - "He'll just come back!" - but Penny has a Spectre coming at her, so she has plenty to worry about. It attacks, but the first attack misses.

Urth jumps down to the ground. ATHL 22. Lands on his feet. He heads towards Kas-Mist and casts Befuddlement - INT 20 save - he fails, but "chooses" to succeed again. 30 damage, even so (all taken). And the he taunts the Spectre! "You're not worth ****!" - WIS 19 save - the Spectre fails and is now drawn to attack Urth in preference.

Francesc tries to attack; it's mist, but an arrow with Lightning Bolt hits instead. 29 total damage, with colossus slayer and hunter's mark. She gets in a conventional attack at (halved) 8 damage (10->5 plus hunter's mark damage 3 being at full).

Well, it's still fleeing, but insofar as a cloud of mist can look "rough" - this mist looks rough.

Thorin-dragon casts Befuddlement too - INT 19 save this time - Kas fails and "chooses" to succeed; his last Legendary Resistance, and it still does 54->27 damage. Meanwhile Penny is shouting - "if we're going to take him down, it'll need Sunlight and I'm _not very fast_..." - hint, hint! - so Thorin flies over to pick her up in his free claw. Penny gets into melee range and strikes - she hits, CRIT - L5 divine smite - 20d8 (!) + 10 - **116** damage!

The sword carves through the mist, which parts as it passes, until *dead centre* there's a giant blast of radiant energy.

There's a very brief moment, where Kas is reforming into Kas - falling out of the sky, burning - disintegrating - turning into ash and screaming, in bright sunlight from the sword, unable to retreat or change form!

All that's left, on the ground, is the Sword of Kas. **He's gone** - the spectre vanishes.

That's the end of Kas!

Penny calls, "drop me!" - and Thorin thankfully flies down to ground level and puts her down carefully. He turns back into Thorin. We approach the sword as a group - aura-assisted WIS saves all round!

* Urth 26
* Francesc 24 (with - DM fiat -  heroic inspo)
* Thorin 25 (with bardic)
* Penny 30

So we all make it; echoes in our head - "Pick me up and I will help you defeat Vecna! Only I can defeat Vecna! I have wounded him mortally before - and this time I will complete the job. But you *must* pick me up!"

"What in the Hells do we do with *that*?!" asks Penny.

Urth picks it up!

Thorin yells, "WHY WOULD YOU DO THAT?!"

Urth is about to swing the sword to get blood, promised to the sword (!) - CHA save, 31 - but Urth resists it.

* Its purpose *is* to destroy Vecna
* Kill all of Vecna's worshippers, destroy his works, foil his machinations
* It has features like Call Lighting, Finger of Death and more...

Eyes wide, he tell us about how powerful it is; he's tempted. He's dropped it, and says "Not for me!" but is, nonetheless (very!) tempted.

We can though literally *talk* to this sentient sword. It's incredibly dangerous and very evil... But its plans are aligned with ours, in many ways. The sword bargains - it'll talk to us if *one* of us wields it...

* Urth d100 -> 7. If he wielded the sword, Urth would gain proficiency Intimidation.
* d100 -> 85. If he wielded the sword, he would be able to cast Force Cage once a day. It's an unusual variant of the spell which, once cast, d6 -> if 6, it can be cast again that day.
* d100 -> 38. If he wielded the sword, and if he was more than 10ft away from the sword, he would become deaf!
* d100 -> 59. If he wielded the sword, then (DM modification on RAW) it erases magic scrolls within 10ft of itself.

"This thing is *fucked*", observes Penny. She checks a scroll she has for Remove Curse... Blank.

"**FUCK**"

"Where's Vecna?" asks Urth. "That's *your* job!" says the sword. "I am here to defeat Vecna, not find him."

The sword claims that we'll use the rod to *put reality back* how it was. We have to drive the rod "into the Nexus of Creation" - Penny, despite herself, calls, "What the hell is that?!" and the sword scoffs. "You all don't know *anything*. I'm not saying anything unless one of you pick me up."

And Urth does it. This seems very bad to Penny.

Urth makes his save, but he knows the blade is at this time very, *very* hungry; it needs blood.

Urth SUR 9.

We are thus treated to the spectacle of a tiny Kender wielding a longsword and running around, swiping at the ground, trying to find critters and animals... But there are none to be found. **Is something chasing them away?**

(Penny has gone quiet at this point)

Thorin asks the sword more about the Nexus of Creation. "You need to break through his defences. You don't know what's in the Nexus, do you. You don't? You don't."

* Various PER checks happen. Each question is a roll, but each roll is accompanied by a CHA save, but with ADV for Urth since he wields the sword.

* Penny remains quiet. She's giving Urth a very strange look, but he's preoccupied with the sword.

* Urth asks a question - PER 22. CHA save made easily.

The sword is probably hiding something *and* it's trying to convince him to hold it - for him and Urth to fight it out to see who is worthy of holding it. PER with self-Guidance, and with Urth's added Bardic Inspiration, 23. WIS save - 18 with Penny's +5. This time that pull to take the sword is really strong.

* Francesc finds it too scary. This thing is just too powerful. She doesn't think she has the resilience to avoid being persuaded by it.

* Urth PER 24, ADV CHA save 23.
